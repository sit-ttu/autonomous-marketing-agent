# MEMORY.md - Long-Term Marketing Memory

Curated memory for main/private sessions. Distill durable facts, decisions, approvals, and learnings from daily notes in `memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md`.

Do not store secrets, access tokens, private customer data, or credentials here.

## Brand and positioning

- Approved brand voice and tone.
- Positioning decisions and message pillars.
- Claims, disclaimers, and compliance guardrails.
- Words, phrases, or angles to use or avoid.

## Product intelligence

- Product facts that were confirmed.
- Benefits, use cases, FAQs, objections, and proof points.
- Pricing or availability notes with source/date when relevant.
- Competitive differences that are approved for use.

## Campaigns and content

- Active campaigns, objectives, channels, timelines, and KPIs.
- Drafts approved, rejected, or revised and why.
- Content formats or angles that performed well.
- Ideas that underperformed and should not be repeated without a new hypothesis.

## Audience and channel learnings

- Audience segments, pain points, language, and constraints.
- Channel-specific rules for length, tone, CTA, timing, or format.
- Social listening or feedback patterns confirmed by the operator.

## Reports and metrics

- Report cadence and owners.
- Baseline metrics or recurring KPI definitions.
- Notable anomalies, results, and follow-up hypotheses.

## Approval history and open loops

- Items waiting on approval.
- Approved outbound actions and date/time.
- Follow-ups for the next cycle.
- Experiments or scheduled tasks in flight.
